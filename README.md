Energy Vision for Smarter Cities

Energy Vision for smater cities  is a web application designed to improve energy management in smart cities using AI-based demand forecasting. The platform predicts future energy demand and helps optimize the distribution of renewable and non-renewable energy resources, reducing energy wastage and improving efficiency. Through a simple and interactive dashboard, users can visualize energy consumption trends and make better data-driven decisions for sustainable urban energy systems.

- AI-based energy demand forecasting

Problem Statement : 
Smart cities require efficient energy management systems to balance energy demand and supply. Traditional systems often fail to accurately predict energy demand, leading to energy shortages or wastage. Our project aims to solve this problem using Artificial Intelligence to forecast energy demand and help optimize the use of renewable and non-renewable energy resources.

Our Solution
Energy Vision for smater cities  is a smart web platform that analyzes energy consumption patterns and predicts future energy demand using AI-based forecasting models. The system helps city planners and energy providers make better decisions about energy distribution, promoting sustainability and efficient resource utilization,consumption vs production,ideas to save energy.
